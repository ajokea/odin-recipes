Ground Beef Tacos

Description

These Ground Beef Tacos are filled with juicy, flavorful taco meat, made with my homemade taco seasoning- it's an easy weeknight recipe for the whole family!

This recipe makes about 8 servings and will take 15 minutes to prep and 15 minutes to cook- done in half an hour!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb 80/20 ground beef
  • 2 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 yellow onion, diced

Steps

  1. Saute the garlic and onions with olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat until fragrant and softend (about 2-3 minutes).
  2. Add the ground beef to the skillet and cook until browned (about 5-7 minutes).
  3. Add the cayenne pepper, cumin, garlic powder, salt, pepper, tomato paste, and water. Stir to combine and continue cooking over medium-low heat until the sauce thickens (about 3-5 minutes).
  4. Serve warm over flour tortillas with toppings of your choice. Some recommended toppings are: shredded lettuce, shredded cheddar cheese, chopped tomatoes, salsa, and sour cream.
